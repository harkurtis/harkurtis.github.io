About Harkurtis
We are a team of long time friends and college students looking to help make the university community a safer place. As members of this community ourselves, we have a special desire for the safety of people on and around college campuses everywhere. Made up from different college backgrounds, each of us brings something unique to our team. We are committed to tackling challenging problems and providing solutions to help ensure everyone can feel safe on and around campus, no matter the time.
